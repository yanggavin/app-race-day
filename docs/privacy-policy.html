Privacy Policy

Race Countdown Version 2.0.0

Our Privacy Commitment

Race Countdown is built with a privacy-first mindset. We do not sell your data, we do not use tracking pixels, and we do not run advertisements. Your data belongs to you.

1. Data Collection & Storage

We minimize the data we collect to provide you with the best experience:

  • Local Storage: By default, all race data (names, dates, goals, results) is stored locally on your device.
  • Cloud Sync (Optional): If you enable iCloud Sync on iOS, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this account or the data stored within it.
  • Profile Information: If you add a profile photo or display name, it is stored locally on your device.

2. Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions only when you actively use specific features:

  • Calendar: Used only when you tap "Add to Calendar" to create race events and reminders.
  • Photo Library: Used only to save generated race cards to your device or to select a profile photo.
  • Health App (Optional): Used to sync your weekly mileage and personal bests for performance insights.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics or marketing SDKs. We rely on the standard, anonymous crash reports provided by the Apple App Store to improve app stability.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only "shared" when you explicitly use the "Share" feature to send a race card or screenshot to others.

5. Children's Privacy

Race Countdown is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect personal information from children or target them with advertising.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can delete individual races or clear all app data at any time from within the app settings. Uninstalling the app will remove all local data.
