Race Countdown – Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 12, 2025

中文版

Overview

Race Countdown (the “App”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we do and do not collect, how any information may be used, and your rights. If anything here conflicts with your expectations, please contact us so we can address it promptly.

Information We Collect

By default, the App does not collect, store, or transmit personal information. The App processes data locally on your device and does not include advertising or third‑party analytics.

If you choose to contact us for support, we may receive your email and the content of your message. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry.

Crash Reporting and Diagnostics

Apple may provide anonymized crash logs and diagnostics if you have opted in to share analytics with developers in iOS settings. We use these reports only to improve stability and performance.

Data Storage and Retention

App data is stored on your device. We do not operate our own backend service for this App. If you contact support, related emails are retained only as long as necessary to address your request.

Sharing and Transfers

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information. We do not transfer data to third parties, except as required by law.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us to remove it.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. Since the App does not collect personal data, most requests will apply only to communications you send us. Contact us and we will assist.

Contact

Questions or requests regarding this policy can be sent to: support@racecountdown-app.newtree.tech.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above. Your continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance.
