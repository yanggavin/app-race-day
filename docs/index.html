üì± About Race Countdown

Race Countdown helps athletes and fitness enthusiasts track countdowns to their upcoming races and events. Whether you're training for your first 5K or your 50th marathon, Race Countdown keeps you motivated and organized with beautiful countdowns, goal tracking, and performance analysis.

Key Features:

  • Track unlimited races with countdown timers
  • Set goal times and record actual finish times
  • Add races directly to your calendar
  • Share your race journey with beautiful screenshots
  • Daily motivational quotes for inspiration
  • Full bilingual support (English & Chinese)
  • Dark mode support
  • Works completely offline
  • No account required - your data stays on your device

üöÄ Getting Started

1. Add Your First Race

  1. Tap the "+" button on the home screen
  2. Enter your race details:
    • Race Name: e.g., "Boston Marathon 2025"
    • Date: Select your race date
    • Location: City or venue name
    • Race Type: Choose from Marathon, Half Marathon, 10K, 5K, etc.
    • Goal Time: Your target finish time (optional)
  3. Tap "Save"

2. View Your Races

Your races appear on the home screen with countdown timers showing days, hours, and minutes until race day. Filter races by:

  • All Races: View everything
  • Upcoming: Only future races
  • Past: Completed races

3. Track Your Performance

  1. After a race, tap on the race card
  2. Tap "Edit"
  3. Add your Actual Time
  4. Set the Status (Completed, DNF, DNS, or DQ)
  5. Compare your goal vs. actual performance!

4. Add to Calendar

  1. Tap on any race to view details
  2. Tap "Add to Calendar"
  3. Grant calendar permission if prompted
  4. The race is added with automatic reminders (1 week and 1 day before)

5. Share Your Journey

  1. Tap the Share icon at the top of the home screen
  2. Choose "Share" to send to friends
  3. Or choose "Save to Photos" to keep for yourself
  4. Screenshots include a motivational quote and QR code!

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Race Countdown free to use?
A: Yes! Race Countdown is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no hidden fees. All features are available to all users.
Q: Do I need to create an account?
A: No account required! All your data is stored locally on your device. This means your privacy is protected, and you don't need internet access to use the app.
Q: How many races can I track?
A: Unlimited! Add as many races as you want. Whether you're tracking one marathon or an entire season of races, Race Countdown has you covered.
Q: Can I edit or delete a race?
A: Yes! Tap on any race card to view details, then tap "Edit" to modify race information. To delete a race, swipe left on the race card and tap "Delete".
Q: Why doesn't the app show my actual time?
A: Actual time is only displayed for past races. Before race day, you'll only see your goal time. After the race date passes, you can add your actual finish time in the Edit screen.
Q: How do I change the app language?
A: Go to Settings (gear icon) ‚Üí Language. You can choose English or Simplified Chinese. The app also follows your system language by default.
Q: How do I enable dark mode?
A: Go to Settings ‚Üí Theme. You can choose Light, Dark, or System (which follows your device's dark mode setting).
Q: Can I export my race data?
A: Export/import functionality is coming in a future update. Currently, all data is stored locally on your device.
Q: Will my data be backed up to iCloud?
A: Not currently. Cloud sync is planned for a future version. For now, your data is stored only on your device. Make sure to keep your device backed up!
Q: What race types are supported?
A: Marathon, Half Marathon, 10K, 5K, Triathlon, and Other. You can use "Other" for any custom race type like ultra-marathons, obstacle course races, cycling events, etc.
Q: What do the race status options mean?
Planned: Default status for upcoming races
Completed: You finished the race
DNF (Did Not Finish): Started but didn't complete
DNS (Did Not Start): Registered but didn't start
DQ (Disqualified): Disqualified during the race
Q: Can I use this app on iPad?
A: Currently, Race Countdown is iPhone-only. iPad support may be added in a future update.
Q: Is there an Android version?
A: Not yet, but we're planning to release an Android version soon! Follow us for updates.
Q: The QR code on screenshots - what is it for?
A: When you share a screenshot, it includes a QR code that links to the Race Countdown app download page. This makes it easy for your friends to discover and download the app!

üîß Troubleshooting

App Crashes or Won't Open

  1. Force quit the app and reopen it
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Check if you have the latest iOS version installed
  4. Delete and reinstall the app (your data will be lost, but this often fixes issues)

Calendar Integration Not Working

  1. Go to iPhone Settings ‚Üí Race Countdown ‚Üí Calendars
  2. Make sure calendar access is enabled
  3. Try adding the race to calendar again

Screenshots Won't Save

  1. Go to iPhone Settings ‚Üí Race Countdown ‚Üí Photos
  2. Select "Add Photos Only" or "Full Access"
  3. Try saving again

Language Not Changing

  1. Change language in Settings ‚Üí Language
  2. Close the app completely (swipe up from app switcher)
  3. Reopen the app

Countdown Timer Showing Wrong Time

  1. Check that your iPhone's date and time are set correctly
  2. Go to iPhone Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Date & Time
  3. Enable "Set Automatically"
